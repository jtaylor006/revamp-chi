Business Mission

Our mission at Chi Dynamic is to turn grueling cardio workouts into fun and interactive sessions to give you a more enjoyable wind conditioning experience.

New Product

After a period of thorough trial and error, the Company is prepared to introduce the following product to the market:

Chi Dynamic: Chi Dynamic is a cardio training machine that shoots out bean bags in different intervals which encourages the user to sprint and retrieve each bag, then run back to put the bag into the machine.

With its innovative technology and functionality, Chi Dynamic is the perfect cardio equipment for any person at any fitness level.

Competition

In the fitness equipment industry, customers make choices based upon affordability, user friendliness, portability and wireless technology, lack of similar products and if the product is single user and/ or multi-user

The level of competition is low as there is nothing like this cardio machine on the market.

The primary cardio machine competitors for Chi Dynamic are the following:

  • Ellipticals
  • Treadmills
  • Bicycles
  • Row Machines

However, we believe that the company has the following competitive advantages:

  • Chi Dynamic is a never-before seen product
  • Virtually can be used on any flat surface
  • Can be used by anyone at any fitness level
  • Mode goes from Beginner to Advance
  • Single user or multi-user
  • Portable/ wireless/ rechargeable
  • Eco friendly

Services

First-rate service is intended to be the focus of the company and a cornerstone of the brand’s success. This is expected to create a loyal brand following and return business.
